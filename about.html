Main Aim of the project

The User-interactive page of our recipe website provides a friendly experience with an easy registration/login page allowing users to create accounts for personalized interactions. Once logged in, users can access a convenient dropdown in their account, offering options for easy sign-out and quick access to their saved recipes. The “My Recipes” section allows users to manage and organize their favourite recipes efficiently. Additionally, our frontend recipe website offers a unique feature, “Food Album”, under the user account dropdown, allowing users to upload images of recipes they have personally tried. The website also features a search functionality, enabling users to quickly find recipes based on specific cuisine. In our home page we will have multiple cuisine tabs like Indian, Japanese, Mexican, Italian. Under each tab users can find a variety of meal categories for users to explore. Meal categories includes recipes for Breakfast, Lunch, Appetizers, Dinner, Desserts, Drinks. Inside each recipe page, users can save their favourite recipes for future reference, print them for offline use, and effortlessly share them with friends and family.
